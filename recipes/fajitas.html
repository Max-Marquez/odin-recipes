Chicken Fajitas Recipe

A simple weeknight meal for easy dinners!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 3 chicken breasts cut into thin slices
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Yellow Bell Pepper
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 limes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour or corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven broiler to high
  2. Slice the bell peppers, onion, and chicken into thin slices
  3. Mix chili powder and salt
  4. Add 1 tbsp. of oil and half of the chili mix to peppers and layout onto baking sheet
  5. Cook until peppers begin to char
  6. Add chicken to the pan and continue to bake until chicken has cooked through and has gained some char
  7. Remove from oven and add juice if 1 lime
  8. Enjoy!
