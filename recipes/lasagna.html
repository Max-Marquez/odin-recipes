Lasagna Recipe

A tasty dinner for the whole family!

A delicious dinner made with a bolognese and bechamel sauce thats sure to satisfy the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 Jar of Spaghetti Sauce
  • 2 Cups Mozzarella CHeese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of All Purpose Flour
  • 1 Package Ready to Bake Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Make the bechamel sauce
  4. Prep the ingredients to assemble the lasagna
  5. Layer the lasagna
  6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes uncovered
