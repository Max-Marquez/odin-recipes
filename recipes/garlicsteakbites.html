Garlic Steak Bites Recipe

A simple dish with tons of flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons of Olive Oil
  • 1 Pound of Sirloin Steak
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1 tsp Fresh Rosemary
  • 1 tsp Fresh Parsley
  • 1 tsp Fresh Thyme
  • 1/4 tsp of chili flakes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 1 Pound Red and Yellow Potatoes

Steps

  1. Chop and mix the herbs/garlic
  2. Chop the potatoes into 1/2 inch cubes
  3. Add 1 tbsp. of oil and 1 tbsp. of butter to preheated skillet on med. heat
  4. Cook for 5-6 Minutes
  5. Remove from the pan
  6. Add additional 1 tbsp. of butter and 1 tbsp. of oil
  7. Add steak and cook to desired doneness
  8. Add potatoes and cook for additional 1 minute
